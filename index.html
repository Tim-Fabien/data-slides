Data Strategy

Foundations for a Modern, AI-Ready Organization

Generic • Vendor-neutral • CC-BY

Executive baseline for vision, operating model, architecture, governance, value.

Vision

  • Turn data into decisions and AI into outcomes.
  • Make insights & models reliable, fast, governed.
  • Build a scalable platform that reduces time-to-value.

North Star: measurable impact on growth, efficiency, and CX.

Guiding Principles

Value First

Platform > Projects

Responsible by Design
